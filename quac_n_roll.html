Quac 'n roll

In this team project, we were assigned to create a prototype of a recipe we had made. Our recipe was Mexican tostadas. In this project, it was important that we designed the app for a specific target audience. We did this by working in sprints and conducting an interview with the target group after each sprint. This way, we worked from a lo-fi to a hi-fi prototype. In this project, I naturally took on the role of group leader. I was also responsible for the design of the hi-fi prototype, creating many of the illustrations, and contributing to the overall prototyping process.
